TEREA Provence Pearl (Terea Provence Pearl) are grape and menthol flavored capsule sticks for IQOS ILUMA. Comes in a burgundy-gray package with a pearl symbol. If you don't press the button, the stick will have a rich tobacco aroma. When pressed, the capsule adds a refreshing grape flavor to the tobacco aerosol. It has no analogues among HEETS sticks.
